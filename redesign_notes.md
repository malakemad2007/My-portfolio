# Portfolio Redesign Notes

## Design Overview
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Primary: Soft Blue/Purple Gradient (#a1c6d6 to #abbeea)
  - Secondary: White/Light Gray for sections
  - Accents: Yellow (#f1c40f) for buttons, Dark Blue for text/icons
- **Typography**: Montserrat, Poppins (Clean, modern sans-serif)
- **Layout**: 
  - Sticky Navbar with glassmorphism effect.
  - Hero section with large title, subtitle, and personal photo.
  - About section with social links (Email, YouTube, LinkedIn).
  - Experience section with category filters (Leadership, Tech, Research, Social Impact).
  - Projects section with card-based layout.
  - Contact section with a simple form and social icons.
  - Footer with copyright and "Developed By" info.
