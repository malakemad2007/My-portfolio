About

I don't just learn—I build.

I've never been interested in doing things the traditional way. I like understanding how things work, breaking them down, and rebuilding them better.

From leading student communities to creating Dear Future Luminary, everything I've done comes from the same place: I see a gap, and I try to fill it.

My work sits at the intersection of STEM, leadership, and creativity. Sometimes that looks like organizing events, sometimes building platforms, and sometimes designing experiences that actually help people grow.

I'm still exploring, still learning, but one thing is clear—I'm not here to follow a path. I'm here to create one.
