Community Work & Trainings

I've designed and delivered training sessions on student activities and leadership pathways, summer programs and global opportunities, and sustainability and environmental awareness. Each workshop was built on a core principle: simplify complexity and enable action.

The Ripple Effect

My goal has never been just personal success. It's about creating a ripple effect where my achievements become opportunities for others. Every platform I build, every student I mentor, every opportunity I curate is an investment in someone else's future.

This is what drives me: the knowledge that meaningful progress happens when we lift others up alongside ourselves.
